### Consultant's Recommended Pairings

If you want the two tools to feel like they belong to the same family, choose a pairing:

- **The "Structural" Pair:**
  - PMO: **Keystone**
  - Team: **Cadence**
  - *Why:* One is the stone structure; the other is the rhythm of life inside it.
- **The "Modern Tech" Pair:**
  - PMO: **Core**
  - Team: **Loop**
  - *Why:* Short, punchy, 1-syllable names are easy to say and remember.
- **The "Navigational" Pair:**
  - PMO: **TrueNorth**
  - Team: **Stride**
  - *Why:* The PMO sets the direction; the Team takes the steps to get there.
