# Foundational Stage Requirements Checklist

## Stage 1: Foundational Readiness

### Leadership & Governance
- [ ] LGUs (Provinces, HUCs, ICCs) formally commit to P/CWHS integration
- [ ] Conduct baseline assessment of LGU health capacity and investment needs:
  - [ ] Facilities inventory
  - [ ] Provider capacities
  - [ ] Population profiling
  - [ ] Management support systems (referral, DRRM-H, surveillance, info systems, health promotion)
- [ ] Create and formalize the Special Health Fund (SHF) with P/CHB resolution and bank account

### Financing
- [ ] Conduct baseline assessment of LGU health expenditures and counterpart financing

### Health Workforce
- [ ] Conduct baseline HRH assessment, gap analysis, and investment needs

### Information
- [ ] Conduct baseline assessment of health information/ICT systems
- [ ] Identify gaps and investment needs in ICT governance

### Medical Products, Vaccines & Technologies
- [ ] Conduct baseline assessment of procurement and supply chain systems
- [ ] Identify gaps and investment needs

### Service Delivery
- [ ] Review existing referral systems and service capabilities
- [ ] Conduct baseline DRRM-H system assessment
- [ ] Conduct baseline assessment of functional health literacy and health promotion needs

---

## Stage 2: System Development & Capacity Building

### Leadership & Governance
- [ ] Organize HCPNs with PCPNs as foundation
- [ ] Formally organize P/CWHS with:
  - [ ] Expanded P/CHB membership/functions
  - [ ] Strengthened P/CHO with ESU and HPU
- [ ] Formulate and approve LIPH and AOPs

### Financing
- [ ] Utilize SHF exclusively for health programs/activities
- [ ] Pilot HCPN contracting using capitation/global budgets
- [ ] Begin implementation of comprehensive outpatient benefit (Konsulta Package)

### Health Workforce
- [ ] Develop HRH plan integrated into LIPH
- [ ] Begin reorientation of health curricula to primary care (AY 2021-2022)
- [ ] Start development of PCW certification guidelines

### Information
- [ ] Establish ICT development plan for P/CWHS
- [ ] Implement functional EMRs in health facilities
- [ ] Start iHISIM Stage 1 (clinical modules)
- [ ] Begin planning for NHDR
- [ ] Adopt national health data standards

### Medical Products, Vaccines & Technologies
- [ ] Develop procurement plan for P/CWHS
- [ ] Institutionalize HTA and establish HTAC

### Service Delivery
- [ ] Update and adopt technical referral system guidelines
- [ ] Formally adopt DRRM-H Program via P/CHB resolution
- [ ] Implement at least one health promotion module from Playbook

---

## Stage 3: Service Operationalization & Sustained Integration

### Leadership & Governance
- [ ] Institutionalize P/CWHS and conduct performance monitoring
- [ ] Use LHS ML and LGU Scorecard for UHC monitoring

### Financing
- [ ] Ensure exclusive SHF use for services/system development
- [ ] Fully shift PhilHealth payments to capitation/global budget models
- [ ] Transition Konsulta into COBP, including GAMOT for medicines

### Health Workforce
- [ ] Ensure equitable distribution and availability of health workers
- [ ] Certify primary care providers via DOH and PRC (within 10 years)

### Information
- [ ] Link EMR systems across PCPNs and referral providers
- [ ] Advance iHISIM to Stages 2 & 3; integrate with PHIE
- [ ] Operationalize NHDR as authoritative data repository

### Medical Products, Vaccines & Technologies
- [ ] Implement interoperable electronic supply/logistics system
- [ ] Fully institutionalize HTA
- [ ] Reduce prices of essential medicines

### Service Delivery
- [ ] Deliver continuum of care with strong primary care base
- [ ] Establish fully functional patient navigation and referral systems
- [ ] Ensure functional DRRM-H system
- [ ] Implement at least three health promotion modules from Playbook

---

## Final Stage: Universal Health Care Realization

- [ ] Guarantee equitable, quality, affordable health care access for all Filipinos
- [ ] Achieve universal improvement in health outcomes
- [ ] Ensure health system responsiveness, efficiency, equity, and quality
- [ ] Fully integrate all local systems into P/CWHS nationwide (target: 2027)
- [ ] Operationalize national digital health ecosystem with NHDR as single data source
- [ ] Embed health promotion and disease prevention, fostering a health-literate population
