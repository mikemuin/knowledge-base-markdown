# National Health Programs and Indicators in the FHSIS

This document outlines the national health programs monitored under the Field Health Services Information System (FHSIS) in the Philippines, along with their corresponding indicators or metrics.

## 1. Tuberculosis Control Program
- TB symptomatics with sputum examination
- New sputum-positive cases initiated on treatment

## 2. Malaria Control Program
- Blood smear examinations conducted
- Confirmed malaria cases

## 3. Leprosy Control Program
- New leprosy cases detected
- Leprosy cases currently under treatment

## 4. Rabies Prevention and Control Program
- Animal bite cases seen
- Clients given post-exposure prophylaxis (PEP)
- Anti-rabies vaccine doses administered

## 5. National Immunization Program (Expanded Program on Immunization - EPI)
- Children given:
  - BCG
  - DPT1, DPT2, DPT3
  - OPV1, OPV2, OPV3
  - Hepatitis B
  - Measles-containing vaccine
- Fully Immunized Child (FIC)

## 6. Maternal Health Program
- Pregnant women with ≥ 3 prenatal visits
- Pregnant women given TT2+ doses
- Deliveries attended by skilled birth attendants
- Postpartum visits conducted
- Vitamin A supplementation postpartum

## 7. Child Health Program
- Pneumonia cases seen (0–59 months)
- Diarrhea cases seen (0–59 months)
- Children with Vitamin A supplementation
- Sick child management (IMCI-based)

## 8. Nutrition Program
- Weight-for-age status of children (Operation Timbang)
- Children with severe or moderate underweight
- Micronutrient supplementation (Vitamin A, Iron)

## 9. Family Planning Program
- New family planning acceptors (by method)
- Current users (carried over, adjusted for dropouts)
- Total number of acceptors per contraceptive method

## 10. Adolescent Health and Development Program
- Adolescent pregnancy cases
- Adolescents receiving health services or counseling

## 11. Oral Health Program
- Oral health consultations
- Tooth extractions
- Preventive procedures (e.g., fluoride application, sealants)

## 12. Environmental Health and Sanitation
- Households with sanitary toilets
- Households with access to safe water supply

## 13. Non-Communicable Disease (NCD) Program
- Adults screened for:
  - Hypertension
  - Diabetes
- BMI categorization (underweight, normal, overweight, obese)

## 14. Health Facility and Human Resource Statistics
- Number of RHUs, BHS, and hospitals
- Number of health personnel (doctors, nurses, midwives, BHWs)
- Trainings conducted or attended

## Total Programs: 14
