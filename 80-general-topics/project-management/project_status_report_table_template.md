## APPENDIX: Status Definitions

**Overall Project Status:**
- **Green:** On track; no significant issues; confident in delivery
- **Amber:** Minor issues present; mitigation in place; monitoring required
- **Red:** Major issues; timeline/scope/quality at risk; escalation needed

**Item Status Codes:**
- **Completed:** Finished and closed
- **On Track:** Progressing as planned
- **At Risk:** Potential delay/issue; mitigation identified
- **Delayed:** Behind schedule; recovery plan required
- **Blocked:** Cannot proceed; dependency unresolved
- **In Progress:** Actively being worked
- **Pending:** Awaiting decision or input
- **Escalated:** Elevated to higher authority for resolution
