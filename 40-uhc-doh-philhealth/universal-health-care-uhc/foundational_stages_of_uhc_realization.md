### Context of "Validated" EMR Systems

The requirement for a **"Validated EMR system"** in Stage 3 is crucial and relates directly to the mandatory compliance process known as **Standards Conformance and Interoperability Validation (SCIV)**.

- All health care providers and insurers must subject their Health Information Systems (HIS) to **SCIV**, or its equivalent, regardless of the system's development or implementation stage.
- A **validated module** refers to an Integrated Health Information System (iHIS) module that has **passed the software standards conformance and interoperability validation** of the Department of Health (DOH), PhilHealth, and the Department of Information and Communications Technology (DICT) regarding minimum scope, standards, and level of functionality.
