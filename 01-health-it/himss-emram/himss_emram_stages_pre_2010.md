# HIMSS EMRAM Stages (Pre-2010)

## Stage 0

- No ancillary clinical systems installed (laboratory, pharmacy, radiology)

## Stage 1

- Major ancillary clinical systems installed (laboratory, pharmacy, radiology)

## Stage 2

- Major clinical systems feed data to a Clinical Data Repository (CDR)
- Controlled Medical Vocabulary (CMV) and Clinical Decision Support System (CDSS) capabilities
- Document imaging systems may be in place

## Stage 3

- Nursing/clinical documentation implemented (vital signs, flow sheets, care plans)
- First level of Clinical Decision Support implemented (error checking)
- Picture Archiving and Communication System (PACS) available outside radiology department

## Stage 4

- Computerized Physician Order Entry (CPOE) for medications implemented
- Second level of Clinical Decision Support implemented (protocol/clinical pathway support)

## Stage 5

- Closed-loop medication administration implemented
- Full radiology PACS system replaces film

## Stage 6

- Full physician documentation implemented (structured templates)

- Full CDSS (variance and compliance alerts)
- Full Radiology PACS system integrated with CPOE via HL7

## Stage 7

- Complete EMR system with data warehousing capabilities
- Clinical information can be shared via standardized electronic transactions
- Data continuity across all hospital services
- Data used for performance improvement and quality outcomes reporting
